Evening Developer Bootcamp

By Popular Demand!

You asked for it – we are delivering! The best of both worlds for people not ready to give up their day jobs. The Evening Python-based Developer Bootcamp is an intense sixteen-week, part-time, hands-on immersive course that combines individual projects and group study to give you the skills and habits you need to succeed in the growing tech industry.

This course is great for updating your skills or adding programming to your skill set so you can future-prof your career and set yourself apart from the competition.

What can I expect at the Evening Developer Bootcamp, and how will that help me become a good developer?

You will learn everything you need to start your career as a developer. This course covers full stack web development and programming. You will learn Python, Django, JavaScript, Jquery, HTML5, CSS3, SQL, how to think like a programmer and important developer practices like agile, source control (git), testing, and debugging. Engaging hands-on individual, group and pair programming practice, as well as personal portfolio projects help you become an excellent developer. We help you get a job by guiding you as you build your portfolio, and by working with you on your resume and job interviewing skills. Once you're close to graduating the course, you will be introduced to industry leaders.
